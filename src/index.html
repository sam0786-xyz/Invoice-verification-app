About Mint

Mint is a Blockchain-powered platform that bridges the gap between businesses in need of immediate liquidity and investors looking to earn returns through secure investments in verified invoices.

Key Features

Blockchain Security

All transactions are secured using Blockchain technology, ensuring transparency and trust between invoice buyers and sellers.

Altman-Z Score Risk Assessment

Our advanced risk detection algorithm predicts the financial risk of businesses using the Altman-Z score and financial data, minimizing investment risk.

For All Business Sizes

Unlike traditional platforms, Mint caters to businesses of all sizes, providing liquidity even to small and medium enterprises (SMEs).

Tokenized Invoices

Invoices are tokenized using Ethereum-based smart contracts, providing an immutable and secure environment for trading invoices.

For Businesses

Unlock the potential of your unpaid invoices by turning them into immediate working capital. Our platform ensures fast liquidity while reducing the risk of financial instability. Here’s how it works:

1. Register Your Business

Sign up on the platform by providing basic business details, bank statements, GST invoices, and ITR returns. We’ll verify your data through our secure process.

2. Upload Your Invoices

Once verified, upload your invoices to the platform. Our algorithm will assess your eligibility and risk profile using the Altman-Z score model and SOM (Self-Organizing Maps).

3. Get Funded

Investors bid on your invoices. Once an agreement is reached, up to 80% of the invoice amount will be released to your account immediately, with the rest transferred after invoice completion.

For Individual Investors

Earn passive income by investing in trusted invoices. Our platform provides a secure environment with verified businesses and accurate risk assessment, ensuring safe returns.

1. Sign Up as an Investor

Create an account and complete the verification process. You can browse a variety of invoices and review detailed risk assessments of each seller.

2. Browse Invoices

Select invoices from businesses of your choice. Our platform tokenizes these invoices, ensuring transparency and eliminating fraud.

3. Invest and Earn

Invest in high-potential invoices and earn returns. Upon successful collection of invoice payments, you’ll receive your principal and interest based on the agreed terms.
